New Delhi 1985

-Namdeo Dhasal

about the author

Namdeo Laxman Dhasal (1949-2014) was a Dalit activist, a Marathi poet and writer. He received the Padma Shri in 1999and a Lifetime Achievement Award from the Sahitya Akademi in 2004. His works include many collections of poetry, two novels and several pamphlets.Dilip Chitre translated a selection of Dhasal's poems into English. He was one of the foremost Indian poets and critics of post-Independence India. He wrote both in Marathi and English. Besides being a writer, he was also a teacher, a, filmmaker, a painter and a magazine columnist.
