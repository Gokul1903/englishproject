about the author

Atulananda Goswami is a renowned Assamese short story writer and novelist. His translations of many English, Bengali and Oriya texts into Assamese, and of Assamese into English have net with great acclaim. He is a recipient of several awards including, the Sahitya Akademi Award in 2006 for his short story collection Seneh Jarir Ganthi, the Ambikagiri Roy Choudhury Sahitya Bota in 1991, the Kumar Kishore Sowarani Bota in 1997, the Katha Award in 1992, the Sneha Bharati Sahitya Samman in 1998 and the Assam Sahitya Sabha Award for his novel Namgharia which has been made into a TV serial. He has published four collections of short stories in Assamese. He currently serves as a Senior Superintendent of Taxes with the Government of Assam and lives in Guwahati, Assam.
